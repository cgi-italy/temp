Insula Perception
=================

*Insula Perception* is available at https://insula.destine.eu.

A key feature of Insula is the ability to find, explore and use a range of data and services related to Earth Observation.

Insula Perception  allows users to discover data and exploit them with advanced analytics.

The images below provide just a few examples of how different type of data (such raster, vector, time series, and volumetric data) can be exploited.
